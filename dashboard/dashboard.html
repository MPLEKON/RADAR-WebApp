Documentation
This project, a collaboration between UniWA and NTNU, visualizes real radar data from FMCW sensors mounted on a Ro-Ro passenger ship.
The goal is to enhance docking precision by tracking backscattered signals during berthing.

Radar operates at 20 FPS within a 50m range, detecting objects with 3D position, radial velocity (m/s), and SNR (dB).
